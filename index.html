My Projects

1. Scrapbook – Understanding the Self

This scrapbook project is about understanding myself as a person. In this activity, I shared my personality, strengths, weaknesses, dreams, experiences, and the people who influenced my life. I included pictures, reflections, and creative designs to express who I am. This project helped me realize my values, goals, and personal growth. It also allowed me to appreciate my journey and understand how my past experiences shaped who I am today.

2. Diorama – Contemporary Subject

This diorama represents a contemporary community setup that shows modern buildings and establishments such as a hospital, business areas, and renewable energy sources. It reflects real-life situations in today’s society, including urban development and technology integration. Through this project, we showed creativity, teamwork, and our understanding of how contemporary communities function. It helped us connect classroom lessons to real-world environments.

3. Booth Stall Selling Fruit Soda – Business Correspondence

This booth stall project presents a small business concept selling fruit soda in a creative and attractive way. We designed the stall with colorful decorations to catch customers’ attention and practiced making simple business letters and communication related to the product. This activity helped us understand how business correspondence works in promoting and managing a small business. It also improved our teamwork and marketing ideas.

4. Robotics / Arduino – Embedded System (WiFi Camera Smart Robot)

This project is a WiFi camera smart robot built using Arduino for our Embedded Systems subject. The robot can move and stream live video using a WiFi connection, allowing remote monitoring and control. We assembled the hardware components such as motors, wheels, camera module, and microcontroller, and programmed it to function properly. This project improved our skills in coding, wiring, problem-solving, and understanding how embedded systems work in real-life applications.

5. Brochure of Ten Types of Shoe Brands – Multimedia

This brochure project presents ten different shoe brands with their designs, features, and target customers. We focused on layout, color combination, images, and clear information to make the brochure visually appealing and informative. This activity enhanced our skills in graphic design, creativity, and multimedia editing. It also helped us understand how visual presentation is important in marketing products effectively.
